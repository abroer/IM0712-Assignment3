Investigate the dataset to find any internal correlations between the features. A value of 1 indicates a perfect positive correlation, -1 indicates a perfect negative correlation, and 0 indicates no correlation. Any correlation close to 1 or -1 indicates a strong correlation and can be excluded from the model. As the below diagram shows there are no perfect correlations between the features. The strongest correlation is between the features "residual sugar" and "density" with a correlation of 0.84.
